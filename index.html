How it all started

In 2077 the nuclear holocaust started after the bombs drop. Moments befor the bomb landed when entered vault 111 own by Vault-Tec. Unknown to the public Vault-Tec plan was to preserve as many american as possible. Every one in the vault 111 was tricked in a cryogenic tubes and frozen alive. after so many years you were awaken to two strangers killing off all adults, and taking all kids... including your wife and son. You are put back into a cryogenic sleep but awake 210 years later after life support fails.

You fumble your way out of the ruin of vault 111 and find the waste land that was once your home. As you make your way around your old town you find your old pal Codsworth. He starts your jurnoy to find you son captors and your wifes killers!
